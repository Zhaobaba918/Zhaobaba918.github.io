Our contribution

We proposed a data-driven learning-based system named DeepMPV+ to verify palmprints on smartphones.

Specifically, DeepMPV+ consists of two main modules, the ROI extraction module and the ROI matching module.

Unlike the strategies reported in the literature, we creatively model the ROI extraction
problem as an object detection problem, which can be naturally and e ectively solved by modern CNNs.
